❓ Frequently Asked Questions ❓

🕐 1. What are your gym timings?

We are open from 5:30 AM to 10:00 PM from Monday to Saturday. Sunday is a rest day.

📅 2. Do I need to book classes in advance?

Group classes such as Zumba and Yoga have limited spots, so we recommend booking in advance at the front desk.

👨‍🏫 3. Do you provide personal training?

Yes, we offer personal training packages. Visit the Services page for details.

🎁 4. Is there a trial membership?

Yes, we offer a 3-day free trial pass for new visitors. Contact us to claim yours!

🏢 5. What facilities do you offer?

We offer cardio and weight training areas, group class studios, showers, lockers, and a juice bar. See the About page for more info.

⏸️ 6. Can I freeze or pause my membership?

Yes, you can pause your membership for a valid reason such as travel or injury by submitting a request at the front desk.

📞 7. How do I contact you?

Visit the Contact Us page for our phone number, email, and address.
